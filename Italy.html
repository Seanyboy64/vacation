Hotels in Italy

The luxury Grand Hotel Ambasciatori is perched on a cliff overlooking the Tyrrhenian Sea in Sorrento. Offering views of the Bay of Naples from its rooftop terrace, it also has a private beach, garden with pool and free WiFi. Decorated with stucco ceilings, all rooms are very elegant and come with antique furniture and chairs or armchairs. Each one has an LCD TV and a balcony, some with beautiful sea views. Hotel Ambasciatori has a private beach with sunbathing terrace. In high season, guests enjoy free access to the beach, as well as free use of deck chairs, parasols and towels. A tennis court is available in the property's surroundings. Restaurant Le Muse serves traditional Italian, Neapolitan and international cuisine. Meals are served in the elegant dining room or on the terrace, both with views of the sea. There are 3 bars, one in the building, one by the pool, one at the bottom of the cliff. Snacks, drinks and cocktails, are available day and night.

Hotels in Italy

Set in a 9000 m² park, Adler Dolomiti is in Ortisei's pedestrian area, 100 metres from the Alpe di Siusi small cable car. Its free wellness centre includes indoor and outdoor pools plus saunas and hot tubs. Rooms are comfortable and very spacious with wood furniture and carpeted or wood floors. All come with free Wi-Fi, a minibar and an LCD TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels. Breakfast at Hotel Adler Spa Resort Dolomiti is buffet style. The restaurant serves Italian cuisine and South Tyrol specialities in a cosy environment. Guests have free bike rental and an outdoor children's playground. Bathrobes and sauna towels are available for free, and the ski deposit is also free. This 5-star hotel provides a daily outdoor programme with guided mountain biking and hiking tours, skiing, and snowshoeing. The hotel offers free parking and is easily reached from the SS242 national road.

Hotels in Italy

On a rock overlooking the Bay of Naples, the Vittoria includes 3 historic buildings set in a private Mediterranean park. The property's entrance is in Sorrento’s main square. A lift links directly to the harbour. Grand Hotel Excelsior Vittoria's La Serra spa is complete with hot tubs and massage rooms. The outdoor swimming pool with hydromassage area is set on a sun terrace with free parasols, sun beds and deck chairs. With elegant parquet or tiled floors, all rooms come with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms offer a balcony overlooking the bay, or the beautiful Mediterranean gardens. There are several top-quality restaurants on site, and an elegant bar with sea views. Entertainment and live music are organised regularly. Breakfast is a varied sweet and savoury buffet. Private parking with valet service is available on site. A pick up service from Napoli Train Station is available on request.
